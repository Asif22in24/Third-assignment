Concept

A house is a building designed for people to live in, typically featuring rooms for various functions like sleeping, cooking, and bathing. It includes key elements such as walls, a roof, windows, and doors, tailored to the needs of its occupants.

Design Process

The design process of a home involves planning the layout, selecting materials, and ensuring functionality to meet the needs of its occupants. It balances aesthetics, comfort, and structural integrity.

Supervision

Supervision of a home involves overseeing construction to ensure the building meets design specifications, safety standards, and quality requirements. It includes regular inspections and coordinating with contractors.

Budget Planning

Budget planning for a home involves estimating costs for materials, labor, and other expenses to ensure the project stays within financial limits. It requires careful allocation of resources and contingency planning.
